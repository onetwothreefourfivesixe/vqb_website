Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information while you use the app.

1. No Personal Data Collection

The app does not collect any personal data, such as your name, email address, or Discord ID.

2. Messages During Game Sessions

Only messages sent during game sessions are collected. These messages are used solely for gameplay purposes and are not shared or used outside of the game session.

3. Game Session Privacy

Game session information, including messages and results, is kept private and is not accessible outside of the session.
